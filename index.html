About Shree Engineering Works

Two decades of precision engineering excellence

Established in 2003, Shree Engineering Works has been at the forefront of precision machining and engineering for over 20 years. Based in Sangli, Maharashtra, we specialize in manufacturing high-quality components for pump and industrial applications.

Our expertise includes CNC machining, pump assembly (PCE/PCH), and manufacturing of precision components like shaft sleeves, distance sleeves, and bearing housings. We are trusted suppliers to major companies including Kirloskar Brothers Limited.
